The Team

Ryan's hobbies include polka, bird watching, knitting, and generally having a good time.

Zach Walker is the webpage specialist. Zach has extensive experience with designing, hosting, and maintaining web applications for businesses and individuals. Zach graduated from the University of Toledo with a Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering in Summer 2009, and completed three co-ops with Fluor. Zach's hobbies include motorcycles, cars, and computers.
